SPECIAL TACTILE TILES

CERA'S HOME Tactile are unique non-fade, non-slip ground surface indicators (vitrified tiles). These indicators provide cues, which combined with other environmental information, assist visually challenged people with their orientation. CERA'S HOME Tactile collection has been designed with an emphasis on reliability, safety, dignity, and independence for visually challenged people. Tiles are available in buff, grey, red, charcoal and hazard yellow. The purpose of the tile is to warn visually impaired people of a potential hazard or danger. The tactile surface should provide a contrast with the surrounding area to assist partially sighted people. Tactile paving (also called detectable warning surface) is a system of textured ground surface found on footpaths, stairs and station platforms to warn pedestrians who are visually impaired. Mobility Research Tactile Tiles are a cost effective solution that are commonly installed within tiled or paved pedestrian only areas. They are generally not suitable for exterior use near vehicle traffic.
