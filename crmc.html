CERAMIC TILES

Vince Blanco
Create a look of luxury in your home when displaying the Vince Blanco ceramic floor tile. With a look that resembles genuine marble, this square tile features a soft grey brown background with dramatic, high-contrast medium to dark brown veining. With a modern matte finish, this floor tile is sure to lend sophisticated splendor to your room design.

Portugalia Patchwork
Mimicking the appearance of traditional handcrafted Portuguese tile, the Portugalia Patchwork ceramic wall and floor tiles bring rich and refreshing character to any space. Featuring a mix of five unique tile patterns in Mediterranean-inspired hues of blue and white, these tiles allow for a completely customizable design.

Zellige Charcoal Chabone
Crafted by hand in Morocco, the Zellige Charcoal Chabone Star matte ceramic wall and floor tile features uneven surface texture and beautiful color variation from piece to piece for one-of-a-kind character. The smokey grey hues of this artisanal tile bring a bold, earthy feel to any space. Coordinate it with the Zellige Charcoal Chabone Cross ceramic tile to create an eye-catching geometric pattern with a unique contrast in finishes.

Alaska
The elegant stone look of the Alaska ceramic wall and floor tile will create a welcoming, refined space. This glossy tile features beautiful brown and winter white coloring with dramatic movement. Inspired by precious marble, this tile lends an air of splendid sophistication to any room.

Now Silver
Create a functional and contemporary space with the Now Silver Natural ceramic wall and floor tile. This matte stone-look tile mimics the appearance of light grey cement, bringing an industrial element to any design.

Agrigento
Creating a one-of-a-kind modern farmhouse or European villa ambiance at home is that much easier when you use Agrigento on the walls or floors. This old-world-inspired ceramic tile combines an authentic, weathered look and an encaustic style with calming blue, ivory and taupe tones. The result is a versatile and artistic tile that turns your surfaces into an original piece of craftsmanship.

CERA'S offers a wide range of sizes, surface finishes, colors, and designs in Porcelain and Ceramic tiles, that conform to high quality standards of durability and aesthetic values.
